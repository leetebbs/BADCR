    The Project

    The Bored Ape Doodles Club Reborn (BADCR) is a collection of 5.555 NFTs reborn on the Polygon blockchain. BADCR is a mirror project of the rugged BADC on the ETH network. The Original BADC project was a rug pull, leaving the community in disarray. BADCR aims to bring the BADC community back together and take control of the future of your BADC's and BADCR's.

    Every BADCR has a specific combination of traits no other BADCR has, making every single one a unique collectible. BADCRs are a combination of two globally known popular NFT projects, creating a completely new collection. Your BADCR is not only a unique collectible but also your key to enter the exclusive club. Members of the club benefit from future drops, exclusive deals and exclusive merch packages. On top of that the club decides the future roadmap of the project through various polls and votes.
